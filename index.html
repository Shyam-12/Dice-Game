HOW TO PLAY

1. There will be two players.
2. You can roll the dice and all the numbers you get on the dice will be added to the current score below.
3. But if the dice shows 1, then current score will be reset to zero and you will lose the points and the second player gets his chance.
4. You can hold the dice at any moment and the score will be added to your main score and chance goes to your opponent.
5. The first player to reach 100 points, will be the winner.
